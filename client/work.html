Lazer

Explore the Manual

Learn about the gameplay! Control your character with arrow keys, shoot in any direction with W,A,S,D. Learn about the different types of enemies, their abilites, how to gain lives, and more!

Ease into the Game

To start off, you get ten lives and will face slow and predictable opponents. Shoot in any direction, if you kill enough people, get to the orb! Watch out for the raining lazers from above!

Expect a Challenge

If you manage to stay alive and build up your score, expect a challenge! You'll be introduced to a different class of oponents with different behavior. Watch out for when they learn to shoot back!

Extra Life!

If you find yourself low on lives, or just trying to plan ahead, grab the orb every ten kills before it times out.

Don't Get Hit

Try to last as many rounds as you can to beat the high score. Don't get hit by the lasers, the enemies, or their blast rays.
